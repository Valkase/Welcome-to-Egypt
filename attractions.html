The Great Pyramids of Giza are a group of massive pyramids located on the Giza Plateau in Egypt.
They are the oldest and largest of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World,
and the only one that remains largely intact. Great Pyramids of Giza

The Karnak Temple is a vast complex of temples, pylons, chapels,
and other buildings located in Luxor, Egypt. It is the largest religious complex ever built in the world,
and was dedicated to the Theban Triad of gods: Amun, Mut, and Khonsu.

The Citadel of Qaitbay is a large complex of buildings, including a mosque, a palace,
and a number of defensive towers. It is surrounded by a high wall, and has a number of gates.
The Citadel is a popular tourist destination, and offers stunning views of the city of Alexandria.
