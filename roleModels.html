Ahmed Zewail was an Egyptian-American chemist who was
awarded the 1999 Nobel Prize in Chemistry for his work on femtochemistry.
He was the first Egyptian and Arab to win a Nobel Prize in a scientific field,
and the second African to win a Nobel Prize in Chemistry.

Sir Magdi Yacoub is a world-renowned cardiothoracic surgeon who has performed over 20,000 operations
and published over 1,000 papers in scientific journals. He is a Fellow of the Royal College of Surgeons of England,
the Royal College of Physicians, and the Royal Society. He was knighted by the Queen in 1991 for his services to medicine.
